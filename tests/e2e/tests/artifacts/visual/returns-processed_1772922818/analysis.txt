# Detailed Analysis of APEX MCD Returns Interface

## Overall Layout & Structure
- **Header Navigation**: Horizontal menu bar with tabs (APEX MCD, SIMULATE, TRANSFERS, REVIEW QUEUE, LEDGER, SETTLEMENT, RETURNS) and "SYSTEM NOMINAL" status indicator on the right
- **Dark Theme**: Black background (#000000 or near-black) with contrasting green and white text
- **Two-Section Layout**: Upper section for input/action (Process Return), lower section for results display (Returned Transfer)

## Color Scheme
- **Background**: Dark black/near-black
- **Primary Text**: White
- **Accent Color**: Bright lime/neon green (#00FF00 or similar) for labels and highlights
- **Success Indicator**: Green highlight bar on the left edge of the success message
- **Input Fields**: Dark background with green borders

- **Font Weight**: Regular weight, with some labels appearing slightly bolder
- **Text Color**: White for primary content, green for labels and metadata
