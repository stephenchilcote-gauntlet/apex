- **Accent Colors**:

- **Border Styling**: Subtle dark borders separating sections

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Appears to be a modern sans-serif (possibly system font stack)
- **Font Sizes**: Hierarchical sizing with large title, smaller body text
- **Font Weight**: Mix of regular and bold weights for emphasis
- **Monospace Font**: Used for file paths and technical identifiers

## Spacing & Alignment
- **Generous Padding**: Ample whitespace around components
- **Left Alignment**: Primary content left-aligned
- **Table Alignment**: Mixed left/center alignment for different columns

## Visual Quality
- **Contrast**: Excellent contrast between dark background and light text
